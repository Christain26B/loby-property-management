Accessibility Statement

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Loby Property Management is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Our standard

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities.

What we do

  • Use semantic markup, descriptive alt text, and clear heading structure.
  • Maintain sufficient color contrast and support keyboard navigation.
  • Respect reduced-motion preferences for animations.
  • Design forms with clear labels and focus states.

Housing accessibility

Beyond our website, Loby provides reasonable accommodations and modifications for residents and applicants with disabilities in accordance with the Fair Housing Act and applicable state and local laws. Contact your community team to request an accommodation.

Need help or found an issue?

If you encounter any barrier on this site, or need information in an alternative format, please contact us at access@lobypm.com or call (669) 303-7340. We welcome your feedback and aim to respond within three business days.
