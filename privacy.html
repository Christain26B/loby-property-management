Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Loby Property Management, Inc. ("Loby," "we," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and resident services, how we use it, and the choices you have. This is a demonstration website; the practices below describe how a real property manager would typically handle data.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide — name, email, phone, and details you submit through our contact, tour, application, maintenance, or proposal forms.
  • Resident & applicant data — lease, payment, and maintenance information needed to operate your community.
  • Automatically collected data — device, browser, and usage information collected via cookies and similar technologies.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries, schedule tours, and process applications.
  • To operate leasing, rent payments, maintenance, and resident communications.
  • To improve our website and services, and to meet legal and safety obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who help us operate (such as payment processors and screening partners), with property owners as needed to manage their assets, and where required by law.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may opt out of marketing messages at any time. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA. To exercise any right, contact us using the details below.

Security & retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, and retain it only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@lobypm.com or write to Loby Property Management, 3550 Wilshire Blvd, Suite 1400, Los Angeles, CA 90010.
